Patch notes

V1.0
- Added many lootable containers that are not normally lootable.
- Added economic values to a few items found in new loot containers coz who doesn't need more dukes!

- Lootable containers
	- Communion table:
		- Toilet water, boiled water, holy beer and moonshine!!!
	- Piano:
		- Paper, polymers, duct tape, wood, brass, lead, leather and mechanical parts.
	- Solar bank:
		- Electrical parts, car battery, solar cells (repairable and sellable to the trader).
	- Urinals:
		- Same loot table as toilets.
	- IV Stand:
		- Blood bags, what else (wish I had a model of the stand without the blood bag, that'd be awesome!)
	- Water barrels:
		- Toilet water, boiled water, mineral water.
	- Water fountains:
		- Same loot table as water coolers.
	- Computers:
		- Electrical parts, scrap polymers, scrap iron.
	- Pet cage and dog houses:
		- Rotten flesh, feathers, raw meat, eggs and nitrate.
	- Brown boxes:
		- A very long list of resources and items to sell at the traders that you don't usually find.
	- Pallets:
		- Same as you would get from digging/mining them up but by looting.
	- TV pallets:
		- Well .... a decent chance of getting a TV or electrical parts.
	- Toasters:
		- Charred meat, sham sandwich, corn bread and pumkin bread.
	- Ice machines:
		- Snow and yucca smoothies.
	- Wine barrels and bottles:
		- Toilet water, beer and moonshine.
	- Military crates:
		- Same loot table as munition boxes.
	- Sofas:
		- Old cash, dukes and paper.
	- Body bags:
		- Rotten flesh and nitrate.
	- More and more to come.
